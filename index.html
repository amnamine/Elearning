Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with a course?

Simply browse our course catalog, select a course that interests you, and click "Start Learning". All courses are self-paced, so you can learn at your own speed.

Are certificates provided upon completion?

Yes! Upon successfully completing a course, you'll receive a verified certificate that you can share on LinkedIn and add to your resume. Certificates are recognized by leading tech companies.

Can I access courses on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones and tablets. You can also download our mobile app for iOS and Android for offline learning.

What is the refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact us within 30 days for a full refund, no questions asked.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students with a valid .edu email address can receive up to 50% off on all premium plans. Contact our support team to verify your student status and claim your discount.

How long do I have access to courses?

With a Pro or Enterprise plan, you have lifetime access to all enrolled courses, including future updates. Free plan users have access for 30 days after enrollment.
